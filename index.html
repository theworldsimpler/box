Exterior platform

Box is conceived as a prefabricated enclosure platform for remote and infrastructure-constrained sites. Its rectilinear geometry, repeatable panel system and concentrated service architecture are intended to reduce component variation, simplify transportation and move a greater proportion of construction into a controlled manufacturing environment.

Occupant module

The occupied volume is organized around a compact service spine and a small number of repeatable interior assemblies. Concentrating plumbing, ventilation and electrical interfaces into defined zones is intended to reduce installation complexity, preserve usable floor area and support factory-level quality control.

Integrated utility systems

The proposed utility module centralizes water handling, thermal management, ventilation, electrical distribution, sensing and control equipment inside a serviceable enclosure. The architecture is intended to support pre-assembly, structured commissioning, remote diagnostics and subsystem replacement.
